Class SkeletonMecanimRootMotion

Namespace
Spine.Unity

Add this component to a SkeletonMecanim GameObject to turn motion of a selected root bone into Transform or RigidBody motion. Local bone translation movement is used as motion. All top-level bones of the skeleton are moved to compensate the root motion bone location, keeping the distance relationship between bones intact.
